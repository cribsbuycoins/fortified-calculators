Sale Analyzer

Investment property sale analysis with estimated tax liability for rental properties with depreciation.

Before You Run This Analysis — Ask Your Accountant

Talk to your accountant and have these answers ready before you begin:

  1. What was the original purchase price?
  2. What were the closing costs at purchase?
  3. What capital improvements have been documented?
  4. What is the depreciable basis for the building (not land)?
  5. How many years have you owned it?
  6. What is your annual depreciation amount?
  7. Am I subject to NIIT (Net Investment Income Tax)?
  8. What is my federal capital gains bracket (0%, 15%, or 20%)?
  9. Should I consider a 1031 exchange before selling?

This tool defaults to Massachusetts tax rates and transaction costs. You can override any assumption with your own local rates.
